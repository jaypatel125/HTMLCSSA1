Pineapple

Description

Pineapples are indigenous to South America, grown between southern Brazil ans Paraguay. They have been grown for many
centuries. Pineapples were introduced to Europe in the 17th century where they were seen a luxury. In nature pineapples are
pollinated by hummingbirds. Today, the philippines is one of the top exporters of pineapples in the world.
